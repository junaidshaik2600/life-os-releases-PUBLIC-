# Life OS — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 27 May 2026  
**App:** Life OS (Android package `com.os.life`)  
**Contact:** @junaidshaik_2600 (or the support email listed on the Google Play store listing)

> This policy describes how Life OS handles information on your device. Life OS is **offline-first**: your diary and personal content stay on your phone unless **you** export or back up data.

---

## 1. Who we are

Life OS is a personal productivity and diary application operated by the developer listed on the Google Play store listing (“we”, “us”). This policy applies to the Life OS mobile app and related update pages we operate.

---

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Content you create (stored locally)

Life OS stores the following **on your device only** by default:

- Diary entries, todos, planner data, people and relationships, reminders, notes, tags, and attachments you choose to add
- App settings (including optional app lock PIN, stored using device security features)
- Local app preferences and entitlement state (e.g. preview trial, Premium status)

We do **not** operate a cloud account or server that receives your diary text as part of normal use.

### 2.2 Optional network use

When you use certain features, limited data may be sent over the internet:

| Feature | What is sent | Why |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| **Check for updates** | Request to our public `version.json` URL | Tell you if a newer APK is available |
| **Download update** | APK file from the URL you approve | Install updates you choose |
| **Google Play Billing** (optional) | Purchase handled by Google | Subscriptions or one-time Premium |
| **Google AdMob** (free tier) | Device/ad identifiers per Google’s ad SDK | Show ads to support the free app |

We do not receive your diary content through these features.

### 2.3 What we do not collect by default

- No mandatory account sign-in
- No upload of your diary to our servers
- No sale of your personal journal content to third parties

---

## 3. How we use information

- Run app features on your device
- Optional ads on the free tier (see §5)
- Optional Premium purchases via Google Play
- Optional update checks you trigger or that use a public manifest

---

## 4. Storage and security

- Primary data: local SQLite database and app files on your device
- Optional **encrypted backup** files: protected by a password **you** choose; we cannot read them without your password
- Optional **app lock** (PIN / biometrics): handled on-device

You are responsible for keeping your device, backup passwords, and keystore of trust safe.

---

## 5. Third-party services

### Google Play

If you install from Google Play or purchase Premium, Google processes payment under [Google Play Terms](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/).

### Google AdMob (free tier)

We may show ads via Google AdMob. Google may collect device and ad interaction data as described in [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [AdMob policies](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543). **Premium and preview trial users do not see in-app ads.**

### Google Play Services

Used for billing and ads SDKs where applicable.

---

## 6. Your choices

- **Delete data:** Uninstall the app or clear app storage in Android settings
- **Export:** Use in-app backup export (encrypted, optional)
- **Ads:** Upgrade to Premium (when available) or use preview trial for an ad-free period
- **Updates:** You choose whether to download APK updates
- **Permissions:** Deny optional permissions (e.g. notifications, photos) — related features may not work

---

## 7. Children

Life OS is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us via the Play listing support channel.

---

## 8. International users

Data is processed primarily on your device. If you use AdMob or Play services, Google may process data according to its policies and your region.
